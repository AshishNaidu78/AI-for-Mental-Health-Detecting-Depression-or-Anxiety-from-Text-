About This Application

Understanding our mental health analysis tool

How It Works

Natural Language Processing

This application uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models to analyze text for potential indicators of mental health conditions. These models have been trained on large datasets to recognize patterns and language associated with various mental health states.

Available Models

We offer multiple language models specialized for mental health analysis:

  • Telugu BERT: Specialized for Telugu language text analysis
  • XLM-RoBERTa: Multilingual model supporting cross-lingual analysis
  • IndicBERT: Optimized for Indian languages
  • MuRIL: Multilingual Representations for Indian Languages

Analysis Process

When you submit text, our system:

  1. Processes the input through the selected model
  2. Evaluates various linguistic patterns and indicators
  3. Categorizes the text into one of several mental health categories
  4. Provides a confidence score for the prediction
  5. Offers relevant resources based on the analysis

Important Disclaimer

This tool is intended for educational and awareness purposes only. It is not a substitute for a licensed mental health professional's advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Privacy Policy

Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Data is processed locally on our secure servers
  • We do not store the full text of your submissions
  • Analysis history is stored in your browser session only
  • Anonymous data points may be collected for improving the models
